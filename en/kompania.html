Hygiene and Health

Organic milk transported to our factory undergoes quality tests and is put into production.

Transportation

"Sharri" products are transported from the factory to the points of sale by van refrigerator. This enables the quality in their composition not to change.

Air filtration

In our production facility the entry of microorganisms and fungal spores is prevented by air filtration.

Organic Content

Organic milk is used in the production of "Sharri" products with no additives used.

Pasteurization

The milk used to make Sharri products is pasteurized and homogenized.

Technology and Experience

Our products are qualitative as a result of our many years of experience, processed with the most modern machinery.

Background Story
Duplicator and lactofreezer

photo no.1

The processing capacity was 150-300L of milk per day received from three farmers. The founders of the dairy are parents Veli and Zyrafete Halimi, and their son Nderimi.

The first products of our dairy were yogurt, clabber and ayran. The products were initially sold only in Prizren and Rahovec, and later in Ferizaj. The demand for these products was constantly increasing, especially for cottage cheese. As demand increased, the need for larger equipment increased. After two years, in 2003, the number of workers increased by 2.

Demands for growth continued, the market was opening in other cities of Kosovo. Thus, in 2007, the number of employees increased to 9. In this period, SHARRI Dairy received milk from 56 farmers from the Opoja area and from the city of Prizren itself.

In 2009 SHARRI was moved from the former building to a new building in the industrial zone of Prizren, with an area of ​​4000 . More modern equipment was installed in this facility than before. The milk was no longer pasteurized in the duplicator, but in the pure it was homogenized and standardized, which enabled the increase of the quality of the products.

Photo line no.2

In 2009, the number of workers was 14. Demand for supply was constantly increasing, and thus the number of workers increased to 16, in parallel with the increase in the number of farmers who brought milk. \developed countries, such as Switzerland, Austria, Greece and the USA. In all these states, one of the staff workers was for training, which is evidenced by the certificates issued by the companies of these states. In 2018, SHARRI competed in MAFRD for the grant which we won. With this grant and own capital, we made investments in the modern line for milk processing.

Photo line no.3

Demand for supply became even greater, the amount of milk increased and so on, the number of workers went to 27. In 2019-2020, processing reaches the figure of 20,000L of milk per day, while the number of workers is 35. SHARRI cooperates with all wholesale and retail trade networks, regardless of nationality. SHARRI supplies and transports products throughout Kosovo. The vision of the company SHARRI is to produce the highest quality products, original and without chemical additives, so as not to endanger the health of our customers.

SHARRI Dairy does not discriminate in employment. The number of employees is almost proportional on a gender basis, and we have employees from different communities. SHARRI Dairy is located in the industrial zone of the municipality of Prizren, in a building of 2200m ^ 2, with a green yard and parking for the vehicles of the company and its employees. SHARRI always takes care of the preservation of the living environment, as a condition to meet the standards required by law. Our products are 100% original, from organic cow's milk without additives and chemical additives.
